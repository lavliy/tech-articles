# Google Rolls Out Gemini AI Update to Improve Android Battery Life

Google has begun rolling out a new Gemini-powered update aimed at improving battery life across select Android devices. The update enhances how Android manages background processes, app activity, and power-intensive tasks using on-device AI.

According to Google, Gemini analyzes user behavior patterns to predict when apps should be paused, restricted, or allowed to run freely. This approach reduces unnecessary CPU usage and helps extend battery life without requiring users to manually adjust settings.

Early testers have reported noticeable improvements, particularly on Pixel devices. Some users claim they are seeing up to an hour of additional screen-on time after installing the update.

The rollout also includes enhancements to Adaptive Charging and Adaptive Battery, allowing the system to better understand charging habits and optimize long-term battery health. Google says these improvements will continue to evolve as Gemini receives more on-device training data.

The update is currently available for Pixel phones and is expected to expand to other Android manufacturers in the coming months. As AI becomes more deeply integrated into Android, users can expect more intelligent power management features in future releases.
